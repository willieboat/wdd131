Willie Car Rental – Site Plan

Willie Car Rental

This name represents a company that provides car rental services in Ghana.

Site Purpose

The website will provide car rental services to prospective customers of Willie Car rental Company, host a catalogue of vehicles available for rental and also have a form for the rental and payment.

Scenarios

  • What type of vehicle will suit my purpose for rental?
  • What are the rules and regulations regarding the rental?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #1e3d59 – Used for headers, navigation background, and footer.
  • Accent Color: lightblue – Used for buttons, callouts, and link hovers.

Typography

  • Montserrat – Used for headings to give a clean, bold, and adventurous feel.
  • Roboto – Used for body text to maintain clarity and readability.
